Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the carb estimate seem off?

Estimates are AI approximations. Photograph food clearly with good lighting. You can also edit the result after a scan.

How do I restore my Sneaky Carb Pro subscription?

Open the app → Settings → tap "Restore Purchases". Use the same Apple ID you originally subscribed with.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → cancel Sneaky Carb Pro. Access continues until end of billing period.

Does the app store my food photos?

No. Photos are sent to our proxy only to generate estimates and are not stored remotely.
