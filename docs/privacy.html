Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Sneaky Carb Camera analyzes food photos so the app can estimate carbohydrate content and highlight hidden carb sources.

What the app processes

  • Photos you capture in the app
  • Photos you import from your library
  • Scan results generated from those photos
  • Optional saved history entries stored on your device

Cloud processing

When you analyze a photo, the image data is sent to Sneaky Carb Camera's managed analysis proxy. The proxy authenticates requests, applies rate limits, logs request metadata for abuse monitoring, and forwards the image to our AI provider. The app does not operate its own remote storage for these photos.

On-device storage

If you save a scan result, Sneaky Carb Camera stores the result details and optional thumbnail locally on your device. You can remove entries from the History screen at any time.

Purchases

Sneaky Carb Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not store payment information.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Image data is shared only with our AI provider during active analysis. No data is shared with advertisers or third-party analytics.

Children

Sneaky Carb Camera is not directed at children under 13.
